Problem Statement

To develop a machine learning-based system that accurately predicts residential property prices in Bengaluru based on structural and locational attributes, addressing the information asymmetry in the real estate market.

About the Model

  • Algorithm: Linear Regression (Scikit-Learn)
  • Dataset: Bengaluru Housing Dataset (Kaggle)
  • Preprocessing: Outlier removal (Standard Deviation), One-Hot Encoding
  • Validation: K-Fold Cross Validation (5-fold)
  • Train-Test Split: 80% Training, 20% Testing

Limitations

  • Model trained only on historical Bengaluru dataset (2016-2017).
  • Does not account for real-time market inflation or macroeconomic factors.
  • Limited feature set (Sqft, Bath, BHK, Location) - ignores floor level, amenities, etc.
  • Price estimates for "Other" locations may have higher variance.
